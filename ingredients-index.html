Plant Oils

Plant oils are fully packed with vitamins, essential fatty acids (omega 3,6,9 ) and antioxidants, which are known as superfood for the skin. They have a lot of amazing benefits for the skin. They are nourishing, softening, soothing, healing and great for skin cell regeneration.

Some of the most popular plant oils used in skincare are:

  • Avocado oil
  • Castor oil
  • Olive oil
  • Argan oil
  • Rosehip oil
  • Jojoba oil
  • Sweet almond oil
  • Coconut oil

Butters

Butters are plant solid oils at room temperature. They are incredibly rich and extra moisturizing, nourishing ingredients that are most used in products for normal to dry skin, eczema and psoriasis. They have a slower absorption than plant liquid oils. They also have long shelf-life of up to 2 years.

Some of the most popular butters used in skincare are:

  • Cocoa butter
  • Shea butter
  • Mango butter
  • Kokum butter

Waxes

Waxes are occlusive ingredients that work as a protective barrier. They are most commonly used in all type of balms and ointments. It works to increase viscosity of the product and has a long shelf-life of up to 3 years.

Some of the most popular waxes used in skincare are:

  • Beeswax
  • Candelilla wax
  • Soy wax
